üì± Method 1: Delete Account via Mobile App

The easiest way to delete your account is directly from the MarketBrand mobile app:

  1. Open the MarketBrand app on your device
  2. Tap on Profile (bottom navigation bar)
  3. Tap on Settings or the settings icon
  4. Scroll down and tap "Delete Account"
  5. Read the warning message carefully
  6. Confirm your decision by tapping "Delete My Account"
  7. Enter your password or verify your identity if prompted
  8. Tap "Confirm Deletion" to finalize

Note: You must be logged in to delete your account via the app.

üìß Method 2: Request Deletion via Email

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

  1. Send an email to support@marketbrand.ai
  2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  3. Include the following information in your email:
    • Your registered email address
    • Your registered phone number
    • Your full name as registered in the app
    • A clear statement that you want to delete your account
  4. We will verify your identity and send you a confirmation email
  5. Reply to the confirmation email to finalize the deletion

üîç What Data Will Be Deleted?

When you delete your account, the following information will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, profile picture
  • Business Profiles: All business information, logos, and contact details
  • Created Content: All posters, greetings, videos, and marketing materials
  • Media Files: All uploaded images, videos, and other media
  • Preferences: App settings, templates, and customization
  • Account Data: Login credentials and authentication tokens
  • Subscription Information: Active subscription will be cancelled

üìã What Data Will Be Retained?

For legal, security, and accounting purposes, some information may be retained even after account deletion:

  • Transaction Records: Payment history and invoices (retained for 7 years as per Indian tax laws)
  • Legal Communications: Records of legal requests or disputes
  • Anonymized Analytics: Aggregated, non-identifiable usage data for statistical purposes
  • Fraud Prevention Data: Information related to security incidents or violations

This retained data is the minimum necessary to comply with legal obligations and cannot be linked back to you personally.

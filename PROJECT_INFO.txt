MarketBrand

================================================================================
PROJECT DESCRIPTION
================================================================================

MarketBrand is a comprehensive mobile application designed for event marketing,
poster creation, greeting cards, and video editing. It serves as an enterprise
marketing suite for ambitious creators, trusted by businesses worldwide.

Key Features:
- Event Marketing Tools: Create and manage marketing campaigns for events
- Poster Maker: Design and customize professional posters with templates and frames
- Greeting Cards Creator: Generate personalized greeting cards with templates
- Video Editor: Edit and compose videos with overlays and effects
- Business Profile Management: Create and manage multiple business profiles
- Template Gallery: Access a wide variety of pre-designed templates
- Festival Calendar: View and track upcoming festivals and events
- Subscription Management: Premium features with subscription options
- Payment Integration: Secure payment processing via Razorpay
- Authentication: User registration, login, and password management
- Download Tracking: Track and manage downloaded content
- Offline Support: Cache services for offline content access

Target Audience:
Business owners, entrepreneurs, event planners, and marketers looking for
professional marketing content creation tools on mobile devices.

Platform: Cross-platform mobile application (Android & iOS)
